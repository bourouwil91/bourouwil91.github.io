GlaciersExperience is a small group experience that offers an easy glacier hike on Solheimajokull glacier with an experience glacier guide and is available all year around. The tour offers a pick-up from Reykjavik or a meet on location
at Solheimajokull glacier. Add even more adventure to your stay in Iceland and come explore this amazing crawling glacier with us.
Solheimajokull glacier is an outlet from the mighthy Myrdalsjokull and is surrounded by some of Iceland's most grand volcanoes and during summertie, a glacier lagoon forms in front of the glacier only adding to the charm of the location. THis popular day tour is the perfect way to get in real contact with the true reason behind the name Ice-land!
